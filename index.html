Our Mission

The VisionBlue NGO is committed to transforming lives by equipping young women, and vulnerable communities with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need for a sustainable future. We believe that every person—regardless of background or circumstance—deserves the chance to rise, learn, and grow. Through education, mentoring, and community-driven development initiatives, we break cycles of poverty and create pathways to dignity, resilience, and hope.

Origins & Vision

The VisionBlue NGO was founded with a clear mission: to support young women between the ages of 15 and 25 and communities facing social and economic challenges in Cameroon. Its origins lie in the commitment to create practical and sustainable pathways to empowerment—particularly for young women and girls.

Academic & Professional Foundations

The work of The VisionBlue NGO is grounded in the academic and professional background of its founder and members, whose commitment spans ethics, community development, and social responsibility. This foundation shapes our evidence‑based, human‑centered approach.

Response to Crises

The VisionBlue NGO emerged during a period of significant social and economic strain in Cameroon. Our response focuses on constructive action—through support, training, and opportunities that foster stability, self‑determination, and long‑term resilience.

Our Journey

June 2018 – First Beneficiaries Identified

Initial selection of young IDP women in Yaoundé for support and accompaniment.

February 2019 – November 2021 – Analysis and Launch of Business Support

Introduction of micro‑grants and coaching to strengthen income‑generating activities for the selected beneficiaries.

January 2022 – Mentoring & Follow‑Up Program

Structured follow‑up and regular mentoring programs to ensure continuity and growth for the beneficiaries.

January 2023 – Continuous session of coaching & Follow‑Up Program

School campaigns and workshops to sensitize young people against drug abuse and promotion of healthy living.

2024 – Back to school programs and packages

Organised back to school programs and handed out packages to support students in their educational journey. Organised regualar picnics and fun holiday programs for kids.

2025 – Expansion to New Communities

Scaling activities to reach more beneficiaries and deepen impact.
